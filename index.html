About Us

We’re trusted by more than 1000 clients

Kapoor Agencies is one of NCR’s most experienced FMCG distribution and marketing company situated in Naraina, New Delhi dedicated to make its mark in distribution of FMCG products. We specialize in food products like sugar syrups, coffee, spices, artisan water, ketchups, and juices to both masses as well as exclusive niches. We provide multi-channel distribution and brand development services for brands in grocery stores, modern trade, air caterers, airlines, departmental stores, Hotels, Restaurants, and Cafes etc. We understand the nuances of retail trade in NCR well enough. We distribute and build retail brands representing a wide range of premium national and international brands delivering a focused strategic approach to our partners business. We are Super Stockists for Monin Syrups, Continental Coffee, Cremica, Blue Pine Water, Dabur India and VKL Spices.

Our Infrastructure

We have a godown located in South-West Delhi from where the consignments are shipped to NCR. We use reliable transportation infrastructure with time bound deliveries. Our team uses stat-of-the art packaging technology to avoid breakages during transit. We are fully geared with express delivery to supply bulk orders at short notice. Our Sales team follows a fixed journey cycle to visit all our retailers once a month to discuss stocks, retails and brand development initiatives.

Sales

Our Distribution Partners and Sales Team are dedicated to grow our business and our brands business. Our category expertise in Foods distribution ensures that our Brands secure premium shelf display and unrivalled trade marketing support, increasing both brand awareness and off-take.
